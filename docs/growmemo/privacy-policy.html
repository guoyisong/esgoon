1. Information We Collect

To provide and maintain our services, we may collect certain information that is necessary for the normal operation, security, and improvement of the application.

1.1 Device Information
We may collect basic device information, including your device model, operating system version, IP address, application version, and usage logs. This information is collected solely for service operation, diagnostics, and performance optimization, and is not associated with your personal identity.

1.2 Crash Reports
To improve application stability and quickly resolve technical issues, GrowMemo uses PLCrashReporter to collect crash-related information, including crash logs and exception reports. These reports are used exclusively for troubleshooting and improving application reliability. They are not used to identify individual users and are not linked to your account.

If collected information cannot reasonably identify an individual, either by itself or in combination with other information, it is not considered personal information under applicable laws.

1.3 Sign in with Apple
GrowMemo supports Sign in with Apple as an optional authentication method. If you choose to sign in using your Apple Account, we may receive limited profile information that Apple makes available to the application, such as your display name and email address (when authorized by you). This information is used solely for account authentication and application functionality.

1.4 Feedback
When you submit feedback through the application, you may choose to provide contact information such as your phone number or email address. This information is used only to respond to your questions or feedback. Providing contact information is entirely optional.

1.5 Future Features
As GrowMemo continues to evolve, additional features may require the collection of new categories of information. If this occurs, we will clearly explain what information is collected, why it is needed, and obtain your consent where required by applicable laws.
